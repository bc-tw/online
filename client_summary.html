Client Background and Current Environment

Client: Best for You Organics Company

Industry: Wholesale distributor of organic produce and shelf-stable items.

Company Size: Rapidly growing, with three locations and a dedicated sales team.

Current Environment:

  • ERP System: An older, on-premise ERP system that is struggling to keep up with the company's growth.
  • Key Operational Challenges:
    • Inventory Management: Difficulty managing produce with short shelf lives, leading to spoilage and financial losses. Inaccurate inventory costing and reconciliation with the general ledger.
    • Sales Process: Salespeople lack visibility into real-time inventory, leading to overselling. The quoting and order release process is manual and inefficient.
    • Purchasing: Manual application of vendor discounts, difficulty tracking purchase orders for negotiations, and improper invoice matching.
    • Financial Management: Complex and manual accrual process for goods received but not invoiced. Lack of control over posting periods, leading to inaccurate financial reporting.
    • Warehouse Operations: Overwhelming rush order process, lack of separation of duties between office and warehouse staff.
